placed side by side in `docs/design/comparison-home-mobile.png`. The final pass
matches the selected Airy Romantic Film direction: dawn mountain photography,
warm ivory haze, champagne typography, compact navigation, centered names,
date, countdown, outlined invitation CTA, and restrained scroll cue.

The hero is the only source-defined screen. Its header, portrait crop,

## Findings

- No actionable P0, P1, or P2 findings remain.
- Typography uses available Chinese serif and sans-serif fallbacks rather than
  the exact unidentified font in the generated source. The optical weight,
  hierarchy, letter spacing, and line height are within the selected direction.
- The clean production hero was generated by removing UI from the source
  visual. The couple's exact position differs slightly from the mockup, but the
  subject scale, palette, light, and veil movement remain coherent.
- The implementation adds an English eyebrow and slightly clearer countdown
  contrast for accessibility. These are intentional product-level deviations.
